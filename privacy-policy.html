App Analytics

Our app collects anonymized error and crash reports to improve app stability, identify issues, and enhance user experience. These error and crash reports are anonymized and do not include personally identifiable information (PII). We use Firebase Crashlytics as a third-party service to collect and process these reports, and by using the app, you consent to crash and error data collection and processing. Appropriate security measures are taken to protect crash and error data, and access to this data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
